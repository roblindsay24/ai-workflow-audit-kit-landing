Common buyer questions

Is this a course?

No. It is a practical workbook/template kit, not a video course or certification.

Does it implement AI for my business?

No. It helps scope and score a first proof. Implementation is a separate service or project.

Does it guarantee grant eligibility or funding?

No. It includes dated funding-fit questions and official-source links to check, but it is not grant advice and does not guarantee eligibility.

Can advisors or consultants use it with clients?

The packet includes self-serve buyer notes and a consultant-licence hypothesis. Do not resell the raw templates as a standalone product or imply official endorsement.

What should a first proof look like?

A good first proof is repeated, measurable, low-risk, reversible, and has a human approval step. If the workflow cannot be measured or safely isolated, narrow it before using AI.
