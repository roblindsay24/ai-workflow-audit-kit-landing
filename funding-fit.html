Nova Scotia AI funding-fit questions for SMEs

Use this before treating any AI program as a fit.
This page turns the kit's dated funding notes into public-safe questions a Nova Scotia SME, advisor, MSP, or consultant can ask before scoping an AI workflow proof. It is not grant advice and does not claim any program is open or that a buyer qualifies.

Question 1: Is the workflow real?

  • Name one repeated workflow that happens at least weekly.
  • Write the current pain, delay, or rework cost.
  • Decide whether success can be measured with time, cost, error, backlog, or response-time data.

Question 2: Is the proof safe?

  • Can the first proof use low-risk, sample, or redacted data?
  • Can a human approve AI output before customers, staff, money, or production systems are affected?
  • Is there a rollback/manual fallback?

Question 3: Is the program current?

  • Check official pages directly before relying on dates, amounts, or eligibility.
  • Keep funding-fit wording as questions, not promises.
  • Do not imply endorsement by Digital Nova Scotia, ACOA, government, CBDC, chambers, or Scale AI.
