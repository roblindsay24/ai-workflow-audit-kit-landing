15-minute pre-audit intake worksheet

Use this before asking about a fixed-scope AI Workflow Audit.
It helps a Nova Scotia SME owner, advisor, MSP, or consultant decide whether the CAD $500 audit path is worth discussing without sending private data first.

1. Business context

  • Business type / sector
  • Team size range
  • Province/community
  • Workflow decision owner
  • Human approver for AI/tool output

2. Candidate workflow

  • Workflow name and frequency
  • Current steps and tools
  • Measured pain: time, delay, rework, errors, missed follow-up
  • One first proof metric

3. Safety boundary

  • Data that must not enter AI tools
  • Sample/redacted data option
  • Required human approval
  • Manual fallback
  • Separate legal/privacy/procurement concerns

Good service fit

  1. The workflow repeats weekly or more.
  2. The pain can be measured.
  3. The team can use sample/redacted/low-risk data.
  4. A decision-maker can attend or review a 60-minute audit.

Not a fit

Production automation builds, grant applications, legal/privacy advice, sensitive-data reviews, guaranteed funding, and guaranteed productivity results are outside the fixed-scope audit.
