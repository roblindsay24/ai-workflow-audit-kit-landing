Nova Scotia AI Workflow Audit: 30-minute buyer checklist

Use this before buying the full kit or booking help.
This checklist gives owners, operators, advisors, MSPs, and consultants a quick way to decide whether an AI workflow audit is worth doing this week.

Minute 0-10: name the pain

  • Pick one workflow that happens at least weekly.
  • Write the team, system, and delay/rework it creates.
  • Estimate time lost or response-time drag without inventing numbers.

Minute 10-20: screen first-project fit

  • Is success measurable with time, cost, error rate, backlog, or response time?
  • Can a proof run on low-risk/sample data?
  • Can a human approve AI output before customers, staff, or money are affected?

Minute 20-30: choose next step

  • Score 7-10: use the paid workbook to scope the proof.
  • Score 4-6: narrow the workflow first.
  • Score 0-3: choose a safer/repeated workflow.

Copy/paste advisor prompt

Before recommending an AI tool, ask the business to name one repeated workflow, one measurable success metric, one human approval step, one rollback path, and one data boundary. If they can answer those, the AI Workflow Audit Kit may help turn the idea into a scoped proof.
